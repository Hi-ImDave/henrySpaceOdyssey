About the Site

Henry's Space Odyssey is a fun and educational website with NASA API integration. Take a look at NASA's Astrological Picture of the Day, or see what the Curiosity, Opportunity, and Spirit rovers have been up to on Mars. Currently in production, please forgive any bugs and check back for updates. Thanks for stopping by!
